Smart Eye Integration

How we automate the treatment process to eliminate human error and maximize efficiency.

1. Sense

Optical turbidity sensors constantly monitor the "cloudiness" of the incoming wastewater stream in real-time.

2. Analyze

Our "Edge AI" unit (ESP32) analyzes the sensor data to calculate the exact milligram dosage of Chitosan needed.

3. Act

Precision peristaltic pumps inject the specific amount of bio-coagulant. This prevents costly overdosing.

4. Verify

Every liter treated is logged to the cloud, creating a "Green Passport" that proves environmental compliance.
